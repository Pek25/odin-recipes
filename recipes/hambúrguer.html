Hambúrguer

A hamburger or simply burger is a food consisting of fillings—usually a patty of ground meat, typically beef—placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll.

This recipe will show you how to do a delicious and simple hambúrguer

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • salt and pepper
  • hambúrguer buns
  • cheese slices
  • lettuce,tomato,and onion

Steps

  1. Prepare and season the patties. Start by dividing the ground beef into equal portions and shaping them into patties. Season the patties with salt and pepper to taste. This initial step lays the foundation for a flavorful burguer.
  2. Cook the patties to perfection. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-hight heat. Cook the patties on the grill or in the skillet, fliping them once , until they reach your preferred level of doneness. This step ensures the meat is throughly cookes and juicy.
  3. Add cheese and toast the buns. In the last minute of cooking,add a slice of cheese to each patty and let it melt. Meanwhile, toast the hambúrguer buns on the grill or in a toaster. This step adds a delicious layer of melted cheese and enhances the texture of the buns.
  4. Assemble and serve. Place the cooked patty with melted cheese on the bottom half of each bun. If desired, add sauce, tomato and onion for extra freshness. Spread condiments on the top of half of the buns, then place them on top of the assembled burguer.

    5. That's it, your homemade hambúrguer is now ready to be served and enjoyed.
