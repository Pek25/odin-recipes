Rice

Rice is a cereal grain, and in its domesticated form is the staple food for over half of the world's human population, particularly in Asia and Africa.

This recipe will show you how to do a delicious and simple rice

Ingredients

  • 100g rice
  • 200ml water
  • 8g salt

Steps

  1. Rising the rice. Before cooking, rinse the rice under cold water to remove excess starch . Place the rice in a fine mesh strainer or a bowl and rise it thoroughly until the whater runs clear. This step helps prevent the rice from becoming sticky as it cooks.
  2. Measuring and adding whater. For every cup of rice, add 1.5 to 2 cups of whater ,depending of the desired consistency. The exact amount of whater may very depending on the tipe of rice you're using and your preferece for firmness. Combine the rice and water in a pot or rice cooler.
  3. Cooking the rice. Bring the water to a boil over medium-high heat, then reduce the heat to low and cover the pot with a tigh-fitting lid. Let the rice simmer gently for18-20 minutes, or until all the water has been absorbed and the rice is tender. Avoid lifting the lid or stirring the rice while cooking, as this can interfere with the steaming process.
  4. Fluffing and serving. Once the rice is cooked, remove the pot from the heat and let it sit,covered for an additional 5 minutes. This allows the rice to steam and become fluffy. Afterward, use a fork to gently fluff the rice , separating the grains. Serve the rice hot.

That's it, enjoy your meal.
