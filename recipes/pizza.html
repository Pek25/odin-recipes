Pizza

Is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, vegetables, meat, etc).

Now the next recipe will show you how to do a pizza with a crispy base, mozzarella cheese and a fresh tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp) active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 tsp granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 4-5 ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough In a large mixing bowl, combine 2 1/4 cups of all-purpose flour, 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp) of active dry yeast, 1 1/2 tsp of granulated sugar, and 1/2 tsp of salt. Mix these dry ingredients together until well combined.

    Gradually add 3/4 cup of warm water and 1 tbsp of olive oil to the dry ingredients. Stir the mixture until a dough starts to form.

    Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead it for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.

    Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.

  2. Prepare the sauce In a small bowl, combine the chopped tomatoes, minced garlic,2 tbsp of olive oil, and 1/2 tsp of dried oregano. Mix everything together until well combined.

  3. Assemble the pizza Preheat your oven to 475F (245C)

    Punch down the risen dough and transfer it to a lightly floured surface. Roll out the dough into your desired pizza shape and thickness.

    Transfer the rolled-out dough onto a greased baking sheet or pizza stone.

    Spread the prepared sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.

    Sprinkle 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.

    4. Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

    Once baked, remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.

    Slice the pizza into desired portions and serve hot.

That's it,enjoy your meal
